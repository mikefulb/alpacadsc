About

The Alpaca DSC Driver allows connecting to a digital settings circles (DSC) on a Dobsonian ALT/AZ telescope and creates an Alpaca service which client software can connect to and track the position of the telescope.

The author can be contacted at mike.fulbright@pobox.com.
